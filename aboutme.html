Academics

Hi there! My name is Morgan Rae Reschenberg and I'm currently a junior studying Computer Science and STEM Education at UC Berkeley as a Regents' and Chancellor's scholar. At Berkeley, I've taken courses in Cyber Security, Algorithms, Data Structures, Computer Architecture, and more. Specifically, I'm most interested in the interface between software and hardware.
I also work part time as a course tutor for CS61C: Computer Architecture and Machine Structures. This is my second semester being a part of the course staff, and I've loved getting to improve my knowledge of the material and assist students in their understanding.
